1 - Thursday

RootsTech Streaming

All day - Huntington Library - Adults

RootsTech is a nation-wide genealogy conference covering the use of technology in genealogical and historical research. The Indiana Room will be streaming the conference's sessions - for a full list of what's scheduled when, contact the Indiana Room.

ESL Conversation Corner - Basic

6:30 p.m. - Huntington Library - Adults

ESL Conversation Corners create a welcoming environment for adults whose second language is English to practice their English conversation skills. Come converse with native English speakers and practice your language skills. This session is for those very new to the language.

Daddy Daughter Hair Fair

3 p.m. - Huntington Library - 4-12 years & caregiver(s)

If you're at a loss on styling your child's hair, this hands-on workshop will help. Come learn some basic hairstyles and feel confident styling your child's hair in the future. Make sure to bring your own hairbrush!
