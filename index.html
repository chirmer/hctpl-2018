Home

Your destination to learn, to discover, to gather, to grow.

The Huntington City-Township Public Library serves the city of Huntington, Huntington Township, and the town of Markle (through the Markle Branch), bringing a collection of over 160,000 books, movies, music CDs, videogames, and more to its patrons. The library also provides free digital eBook and eAudio content through the Northeast Indiana Digital Library consortium, hands-on and one-on-one instruction through a variety of workshops and classes, free access to a variety of online tools and resources, local history and genealogy support through the Indiana Room, and more.
